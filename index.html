About me

I am a maître de conférences (associate professor) at Sorbonne Université and member of the Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions (LJLL, Paris, France). I am also member of the COMMEDIA project-team at Inria Paris. My main research interests focus on the mathematical study of fluid-structure interaction problems with bio-medical applications and on the developpement of numerical methods to solve transmission problems.

I was previously a postdoctoral researcher at École Polytechnique with Aline Lefebvre. I did my PhD in the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d'Orsay (LMO) at Université Paris-Saclay under the supervision of Astrid Decoene, Sébastien Martin and Bertrand Maury.
